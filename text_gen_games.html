Introduction and Motivation

This project, undertaken during a government mandated two-week quarantine, combines text generation modeling with analysis of the video game market. Having been away from video games for a while, I wanted to get a sense of the market over the last few years. I also wanted to explore simple generative models, and generating video game synopses seemed like a fun application to pursue.
