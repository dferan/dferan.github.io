About Me

The Who

My name is Darragh, and I am a Information Studies (MISt) student at McGill University graduating in Spring 2022. I spent several years working abroad as an ESL/EFL instructor before turning to data science and information management. I started out learning programming and data analysis on my own while working full-time and eventually decided to enroll at McGill and focus on data science full time.

My academic and professional interests are mostly centered around data analysis, machine learning, web design and web scraping, and content and knowledge management (admittedly, kind of casting a wide net here).
